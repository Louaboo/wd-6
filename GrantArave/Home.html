Welcome To Overwatch

This game is a fps and has different classes that you can play as. There are four categories: Offense, Defense, Tank, and Support. These categories have different characters that you can play as. All characters have different abilities and have different advantages and disadvantages to bring to the team.

Competitive

Competitive is a gamemode where people area are playing for the win not for the fun basically. This gamemode gives poeple rank which puts you with other people around your rank. This rank also gives you, at the end of the season, competitive points which gets you a golden gun. Also with every game you play and win you get competitive points and you get SR. SR are points that increase or decrease, depending on win or lose, your rank. Now because of the rank system there are 8 different categories for rank. These categories give you different a amount of competitive points at the end of the season shown by the table below. You also get competitive points for every game you play.
