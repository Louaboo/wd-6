Medium's

All this information is from purely buying the ship and not putting and modules on it. Usually the bigger the ship the more armour and shields. Also the costs are for only the base ship.

Type 6: It is the second Freighter ship in the game. It is a vast improvement to the Hauler. More cargo, jump range, armor and has an extra small hardpoint with 2. It costs 1,045,945 credits. It is a very weak ship in terms of firepower. Most people skip this ship and go to another one.

Keelback: The Keelback is a Combat and Freighter ship. It is better overall than the Type 6. It only has slightly less jump range, cargo space, and manevrability. It costs 3,048,000 credits.

Asp Scout: The Asp Scout is the first Combat Explorer medium ship. It has pretty good jump range. It is quite maneuvarble. It has fairly decent armour and shields. It costs 3,862,130 credits.

Asp Explorer: The Asp Explorer is a direct upgrade from the Asp Scout. Yet, it is a multipurose instead of just a combat explorer. It has more jump range, cargo capacity, speed and boost, armour and shields. It does have less maneuvrability. It costs 6,661,153 credits.

Federal Dropship: The Federal Dropship is a multipurpose ship, and the first Federal ship you can own. It has some of the worst maneuvrablility in the game. It does have some good firepower with 4 medium and 1 large hardpoints. It costs 14,314,210 credits.

Federal Assualt Ship: The Federal Assualt Ship is an upgrade to the Dropship in terms of combat. It is very maneuvrable. It has some serious firepower with 2 medium but 2 large hardpoints. It has the same armour as the Dropship but more shields. It is the second Federal ship you can own. It costs 19,814,205 credits.

Federal Gunship: The Federal Gunship is the third Federal ship you can own. It is an upgrade to the Assualt ship in terms of firepower. It has 2 small, 4 medium and 1 large hardpoints. It has less maneuvrability then the Assualt ship, but more armour and shields. It cost 35,814,210 credits. The Federal dropship, Federal Assualt ship, and the Federal Gunsip all use the same body just with different variations.

Fer-de-Lance: The Fer-de-Lance, or fdl, is a combat ship very similar to the Federal ship. It is not faction owned. It is one of the best combat ships in the game, and probably tied with the best medium ship with the Assualt ship in terms of combat. It has 4 medium and 1 large hardpoints. It costs 51,567,040 credits.

Python: The Python is the last medium ship. It is the largest medium ship in the game. It is one of the more larger ships but it still can land on a medium landing pad, and thus on outposts. It costs 56,978,179 credits.
