Large's

All this information is from purely buying the ship and not putting and modules on it. Usually the bigger the ship the more armor and shields. Also the costs are for only the base ship.

Type 7: The Type 7 is the upgraded version of the Type 6. It is much better than the Type 6. It holds more cargo, has much better armor and about the same shields. It only has 2 more small hardpoints than the Type 6 with 4. It costs 14,472,252 credits.

Imperial Clipper: The Imperial Clipper is pretty much a direct upgrade from the Imperial Courier. Yet, it is 10 times better. It can hold much more cargo, travel slightly farther in jump range, much more armor yet less shields. It 2 medium and 2 large hardpoints, which is much better than the 3 small hardpoints the Courier has. It costs 22,295,860 credits.

Orca: The Orca is the first passenger ship in the game. It excels at taking VIP's to far away systems. Which is the most profitable missions at this time. It does not do too well in combat or trading and okay in trading. It does have 2 medium and 1 large hardpoints and pretty good armor and shields, but it's maneuvrability is horrible. It costs 48,539,887 credits.

Type 9: The Type 9, also called Type 9 Heavy, is the widdest ship in the game. It is the largest freighter in the game and is third for holding the most cargo. It is an upgrade from the Type 7 with better speed and boost, more cargo, more armor and slightly more shields. It costs 76,555,842 credits.

Beluga Liner: The Beluga Liner is the bigger version of the orca. It has 5 medium hardpoints It has more jump range more cargo overall. It is the second and last only passenger. It costs 84,532,780 credits.
